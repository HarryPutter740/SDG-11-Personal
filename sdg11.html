Sustainable Development Goal 11

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Why SDG 11 Matters

By 2050, two-thirds of humanity will live in urban areas. SDG 11 aims to ensure that cities are safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable for all. This includes improving housing, transportation, green spaces, and disaster resilience.

  • Urbanization: Over half the world’s population lives in cities, making urban sustainability crucial for global well-being.
  • Challenges: Rapid urban growth leads to slums, pollution, congestion, and vulnerability to disasters.
  • Opportunities: Well-planned cities can drive innovation, economic growth, and improve quality of life.

Achieving this goal is critical not just for urban dwellers but for the planet. Sustainable cities reduce carbon footprints, preserve natural habitats, and promote social equity. By focusing on smart urban planning, we can create communities that are not only economically vibrant but also environmentally responsible and socially inclusive for generations to come.

SDG 11 in Action

Singapore's Green Plan 2030

A nationwide movement to advance Singapore's national agenda on sustainable development. It charts ambitious and concrete targets, seeking to change the way people live, work, study and play, while creating new opportunities for growth.

Freiburg's Vauban District, Germany

A world-renowned example of sustainable urban living. The district is car-free, with extensive cycling networks, and buildings constructed to ultra-low energy consumption standards ("Passivhaus"), many with solar installations.

Curitiba's Bus Rapid Transit (BRT)

Pioneered in Brazil, Curitiba's BRT system is a model for efficient, high-capacity public transport. Its dedicated bus lanes, pre-payment stations, and iconic tube-like stops have inspired similar systems worldwide, reducing congestion and pollution.

Core Objectives

Safe Housing

Upgrade slums and ensure access to adequate and affordable housing for all.

Sustainable Transport

Provide safe, affordable, and accessible transport systems, especially public transit.

Protect Heritage

Strengthen efforts to protect and safeguard the world's cultural and natural heritage.

Green Spaces

Ensure universal access to safe, inclusive, and accessible green and public spaces.

How You Can Help

  • Champion Green Spaces. Support local parks, community gardens, and tree-planting initiatives. Green spaces are vital for air quality, biodiversity, and public health.
  • Use Sustainable Transport. Opt for public transportation, cycling, or walking whenever possible. Reducing car dependency lowers emissions and eases traffic congestion.
  • Advocate for Smart Policies. Voice your support for affordable housing, efficient waste management, and inclusive urban planning at local government meetings. Your voice matters in shaping your city's future.
